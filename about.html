About

Hi there! My name is Cătălina, I live in Bucharest, Romania and I am a final year student at Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, University of Bucharest. I'm a hard-working person, always looking forward for new opportunities to improve my skills. In the last couple of years, with passion and dedication, I've been drawing League of Legends champions, creating personal traditional/ digital artwork and designing vector illustrations.

I wanted to find a way to combine a little bit of everything I was good at drawing, computer science and mathematics.  I didn't know exactly what I was looking for until recently when I finally discovered graphic design, UI/UX design and web development. Now, I spend my time learning new and interesting things every day from online courses and tutorials about art and design that I put into practice by creating drawings, illustrations and logos. My goal is to develop professionally with the help of a mentor and to further enhance my skills.
